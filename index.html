About Our Institute

Nurturing Tomorrow's Tech Leaders

RG Institute of IT & Engineering was established in 2010 with a singular vision — to conduct state-of-the-art IT training primarily for students from all walks of life in Jhansi. As an authorised NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology) Facilitation Centre, we offer government-recognised certifications trusted by employers nationwide.

Our philosophy is rooted in the words: "Educate and raise the masses and thus alone a nation is possible." Our state-of-the-art labs, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships create an environment where students don't just learn — they innovate, collaborate, and prepare for rewarding careers in IT and Engineering.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions, courses, and campus life.

CCC is the flagship NIELIT IT literacy programme recommended by the National Task Force on IT. It teaches practical computing skills — personal/business letters, internet browsing, email, presentations, and small database management. It's designed for the common man to enjoy the benefits of the digital world.
The CCC course duration is 2 months (80 hours). The NIELIT examination fee is ₹1,000 (plus applicable service tax). The exam consists of 100 objective-type questions to be attempted in 90 minutes online — no negative marking. Minimum passing score is 50%.
ACC, BCC, and CCC have no minimum educational qualification — anyone can apply. CCC+ requires Class X pass, and ECC requires Class XII pass. For O Level and A Level diploma courses, the institute will advise you based on your background.
No, there is no minimum or maximum age limit for appearing in NIELIT certificate examinations like CCC. It is open to students, housewives, working professionals, senior citizens — anyone who wishes to become computer literate.
You need: (1) High School Marksheet, (2) Intermediate Marksheet (optional for some courses), (3) Government-issued ID with photo (Aadhar, Voter ID, etc.), and (4) Two recent passport-size photographs. Photocopies are sufficient at the time of enquiry.
O Level is a NIELIT diploma equivalent to a Foundation Level in IT, recognised by MHRD for employment purposes. It covers IT Tools, Internet & Web Design, C Language, .NET, Multimedia, and ICT Resources. Career options include Junior Programmer, EDP Assistant, Web Designer, and Lab Demonstrator. Exams are held every January and July.
The CCC exam is conducted entirely in online mode — no pen, pencil, or paper is required. It has 100 objective-type questions of 1 mark each, with 90 minutes to complete. There is no negative marking. The exam centre is typically allotted by NIELIT to the candidate.
Yes! NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology) is a Government of India body under the Ministry of Electronics & IT. All NIELIT certificates and diplomas carry national recognition and are widely accepted for government jobs, private employment, and higher education purposes.
New batches begin on the 1st and 15th of every month. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening batches to suit students and working professionals. Weekend batches are also available for select courses. Contact us to know current batch schedules.
Yes! You can apply again in another exam cycle if you fail. There is no limit on the number of attempts. Interestingly, even if you have already passed the CCC exam, you may apply again in another cycle. However, you cannot submit more than one exam form per cycle.
